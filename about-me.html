About me

Hello, My name is Lou, I'm a trans woman.
I am currently taking Creative Computing at the University Of Portsmouth, this website was created as part of the Introduction to Interactive Development module.
Some of my interests include computing and video games.
Some information about me, as young as I can remember I've known my gender identity did not align with what I was assigned at birth, when I was 12 I came out as non-binary, but when I was 15/16 I realised that wasn’t the right label for me, so since then I’ve known I was trans, but due to where I was living at the time, it wasn’t safe for me to come out. However since I moved to Portsmouth and I’ve been living my life as a woman, I’ve never been happier. Ive also known since I was 12 that my sexuality is Pansexual.
