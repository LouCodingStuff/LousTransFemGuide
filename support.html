Support

Welcome to the support page, this page is dedicated to those who might need someone to reach out to, or need help on anything ranging from suicide to identity.

LGBT+ Switchboard

0800 0119 100

Lines Open 10am-10pm Everyday

Ran soley by people who identity as LGBT+, this is a LGBT+ friendly helpline to discuss anything, including sexuality, gender identity, sexual health and emotional well-being.

Childline (Under 19)

0800 1111

Lines Open 24/7

Childline is a confidential and toll-free helpline in the United Kingdom, providing support and guidance to children and young people facing various challenges and issues.

Papyrus (Under 35)

0800 068 4141

Lines Open 24/7

Papyrus is a support line in the UK dedicated to providing assistance and resources to individuals, especially young people, dealing with issues related to suicide, self-harm, and emotional well-being.
